MY WORKS

Thuy Ta ice-cream (ver. 1)

Thuy Ta is a legacy brand of Hanoi, where their name has been resonating over 60 years to many Northern Vietnamese generations. Ice-creams are their leading products, among which stands out the most symbolic one: The Lemon-Mint ice-cream.

This prototype logo features the spiral hollow line running around the ice-cream, connected to the beige retro custom-lettering that resembles hand-painted signs of Old Hanoi in the 60s. Key background color is the mixed shade between the product's 2 main colors: Lime & Mint.

Those spiral lines form a stripe pattern which can be incorporated into Thuy Ta's current paper box packaging to reinforce brand identity.

Thuy Ta ice-cream (ver. 2)

Thuy Ta is currently facing harsh competition from foreign brands, as well as from Trang Tien - the main domestic competitor who just got their brand re-designed. Therefore it is recommended that they should re-designed their identity to regain market share.

Same color scheme as the first version, this logo is made up of a series of thick & thin lines. The brand name is custom-lettered within an imaginary ribbon shape, which resembles the spiral hollow lines of the ice-cream.

A playful spirit & a free-flowing visual sense is expected to convey to young customers. Hence, script letters are chosen, and short converging lines are added around. The first word's two letters are aligned to the ice-cream shape, which helps reinforce the unity of the whole.
